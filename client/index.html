FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI-powered personal finance tracker.

Sign up or log in, choose your subscription plan, then securely connect your bank accounts via Nordigen. The app automatically fetches and categorizes your transactions using AI.
We use AI from OpenRouter to analyze your bank transactions and categorize them accurately. All your data is stored securely in our database for easy access and insights.
In Analytics, you can view your categorized transactions with detailed visualizations. In Budget, set spending limits for categories, and get notified by email whenever you approach or exceed those limits.
You can chat with the AI assistant anytime to get personalized financial insights, recommendations, and answers based on your transaction data and spending habits.
Your data is secured with JWT authentication and encrypted passwords. We follow best security practices to keep your financial info safe and private.
We connect to your banks via Nordigen’s secure API, giving read-only access to fetch transactions without storing your bank credentials.
